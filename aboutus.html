About Us

Our project is a digital Grievance Reporting System designed to assist citizens in reporting issues related to Road Infrastructure, Child Labour, and Garbage & Waste Management. We aim to make the process of reporting public grievances easy, quick, and transparent.

Through this platform, citizens can report potholes or damaged roads, unsafe child labour, or garbage-filled areas simply by uploading an image. The system analyzes the image and automatically assigns a priority to the complaint, forwarding it to the relevant department for timely action.
